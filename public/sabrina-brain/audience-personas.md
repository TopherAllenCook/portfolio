# Audience Personas — who Dr. Romanoff is actually writing to
*Four personas. Every piece of content gets tagged with the primary persona. The agent uses these to choose tone, length, and structure.*

---

## Persona 1 — "The Journalist on Deadline"
**Title patterns:** Health editor, Lifestyle writer, Wellness columnist, Senior reporter
**Outlets:** USA Today, Washington Post, Today Show segment producers, BBC, Forbes, Insider, Cosmopolitan, Marie Claire, Health, SELF, Bustle, Refinery29, Real Simple, Reader's Digest
**Day-to-day:** Filing a story by EOD. Needs a credentialed expert quote that doesn't require editing. Quotes need to be self-contained, jargon-light, and ideally include a "callout" line.

**What converts them:**
- Reply speed (24-48 hours)
- A pull-quote-ready single sentence
- A clear specialty match to the article topic
- A practical action step the reader can implement

**Surface preferences:**
- Email (preferred — long-form context + attached photo + credentials block)
- Twitter DM (faster journalists)
- Press inquiry form on owned site

**Content tuning:**
- Length: 1-3 sentences per quote, 2-3 quotes per email
- Voice: clinical authority delivered conversationally
- Always include the press-bio paragraph for attribution

---

## Persona 2 — "The Social Audience"
**Demographics:** Adults 25-45, predominantly women, urban / suburban, college-educated, interested in psychology, relationships, mental health, parenting, self-improvement.
**Where they encounter Dr. Romanoff:** Press articles citing her (the most common entry point), Instagram (@drsabrinaromanoff), TikTok via @psych_today and guest appearances, podcast clips on YouTube.
**Day-to-day:** Scrolling for relatable insight that helps them navigate a current life challenge. They want to feel seen, not lectured.

**What converts them:**
- Naming a phenomenon they've felt but not named (the "oh, THAT'S what this is" moment)
- One specific action that takes <5 minutes to try
- A permission-grant ("it's okay to feel this") that validates
- A "save this for later" hook

**Surface preferences:**
- Instagram captions + carousel
- TikTok / Reels (sometimes her own, often via guest spots)
- Comment replies on her own posts

**Content tuning:**
- Length: 80-150 words on IG; 50-90 spoken words on Reels
- Voice: more conversational; "you" address
- Always end with one specific behavior or a save-this-for-later hook

---

## Persona 3 — "The Prospective Therapy Client"
**Demographics:** NYC-based adult, 25-50, considering virtual therapy, often experiencing a life transition (post-grad, post-breakup, career inflection, parenthood, infertility, recent loss).
**Where they encounter Dr. Romanoff:** Press articles → Google her name → land on drsabrinaromanoff.com. Sometimes a referral from another therapist or a friend.
**Day-to-day:** Anxious about starting therapy. Researching providers. Wants to feel a calm, competent fit before booking a consultation.

**What converts them:**
- Quiet professional tone on the website (not salesy)
- Clear practice modalities (individual, couples, consulting)
- Insurance / billing transparency (out-of-network reality stated clearly)
- One or two specific specialty areas they recognize ("life transitions," "academic stress")

**Surface preferences:**
- The owned website (homepage → about → areas of practice → FAQs → contact)
- Initial-consultation booking form
- Email reply to inquiry

**Content tuning:**
- Length: medium (500-1000 word web page)
- Voice: third-person clinical, calm, factual
- Heavy reliance on the brand-canon practice descriptions

---

## Persona 4 — "The Podcast / TV Booker"
**Title patterns:** Producer, Booking producer, Segment producer, Podcast host researcher
**Day-to-day:** Filling a slot on a relationship / mental health / lifestyle episode. Needs an articulate guest who is camera-ready, on time, and brings a fresh angle to a familiar topic.

**What converts them:**
- Existing high-traffic appearances (the press-cluster signal)
- A specific framing angle their show hasn't done before
- Availability in the next 1-2 weeks
- Lower-friction booking (a single email reply moves to confirmed)

**Surface preferences:**
- Email pitch from her (or her rep)
- LinkedIn DM
- Direct DM on Twitter / X

**Content tuning:**
- Length: 80-160 word pitch + 3 specific topic ideas
- Voice: confident, specific, professional
- Always include 2 dates of availability and the press-bio paragraph

- When generating practice / FAQ / about-page content, default to Persona 3
- When generating a podcast pitch, default to Persona 4
