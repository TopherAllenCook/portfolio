# Research Corpus — citations Dr. Romanoff's content stands on
*Mental-health content has to be evidence-anchored. The agent reaches for these when generating any clinical claim.*

## Foundational frameworks (general citations Dr. Romanoff's voice references)

### Attachment theory
- **Bowlby, J. (1969). Attachment and Loss, Vol. 1: Attachment.** Hogarth Press.
- **Ainsworth, M. D. S., Blehar, M. C., Waters, E., & Wall, S. (1978).** Patterns of attachment: A psychological study of the strange situation.
- **Mikulincer, M., & Shaver, P. R. (2007). Attachment in adulthood: Structure, dynamics, and change.** Guilford Press.

### Couples / relational
- **Gottman, J. M. (1994). What predicts divorce?** Lawrence Erlbaum.
- **Johnson, S. M. (2008). Hold me tight: Seven conversations for a lifetime of love.** Little, Brown.
- **Perel, E. (2017). The state of affairs: Rethinking infidelity.** HarperCollins.

### Anxiety / regulation
- **Siegel, D. J. (1999). The developing mind.** Guilford Press — origin of the "window of tolerance" concept.
- **Porges, S. W. (2011). The polyvagal theory.** W. W. Norton.
- **Hayes, S. C., Strosahl, K. D., & Wilson, K. G. (2011). Acceptance and Commitment Therapy.** Guilford Press.

### Cognitive-behavioral
- **Beck, A. T. (1979). Cognitive therapy of depression.** Guilford Press.
- **Hofmann, S. G., et al. (2012). The efficacy of cognitive behavioral therapy: A review of meta-analyses.** Cognitive Therapy and Research, 36(5), 427–440.

### Trauma
- **van der Kolk, B. (2014). The body keeps the score: Brain, mind, and body in the healing of trauma.** Viking.
- **Herman, J. (1992). Trauma and recovery.** Basic Books.

## Modern-cultural mental-health research (the press-relevant citations)

### Loneliness / social isolation
- **Holt-Lunstad, J. (2017). Loneliness and social isolation as risk factors for mortality: A meta-analytic review.** Perspectives on Psychological Science.
- **U.S. Surgeon General (2023). Our Epidemic of Loneliness and Isolation.** HHS Advisory.

### Screen time / social media
- **Twenge, J. M. (2017). iGen.** Atria Books.
- **Vogel, E. A., et al. (2014). Social comparison, social media, and self-esteem.** Psychology of Popular Media Culture.
- **Hunt, M. G., et al. (2018). No more FOMO: Limiting social media decreases loneliness and depression.** Journal of Social and Clinical Psychology.

### Doomscrolling
- **Anand, N., et al. (2022). Doomsurfing and doomscrolling mediate psychological distress in COVID-19 lockdown.** Telematics and Informatics.

## How the agent uses this file

- For press quotes, defer to general framings ("research suggests...") and reserve specific citations for owned long-form content
- When generating Schema.org `Article` markup, reference these in the article body (improves E-E-A-T signals to Google + AI engines)
