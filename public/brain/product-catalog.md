# Product Catalog — Terpene Belt Farms
*Extracted from the public compliance / COA sitemap and shop categories. Agent reads this to reference real SKUs by name instead of inventing them.*

## Naming convention
SKUs are named `<Category>-<Number>` (e.g., `Fruit-9`, `Gas-707`, `Pine-37`, `Dessert-109`, `Sweet-16`).
- The number = TBF's internal cultivar/profile ID; it is stable across vintages
- Category prefix = aroma family
- Year prefix on COAs (e.g., `2024-Sweet-16`) = harvest vintage
- Roman-numeral "Season V / VI" notation appears in About/PR but not in SKU IDs

## Aroma families (the 8 canonical categories)

### 🍓 Fruit
Bright, citrus-adjacent, often Pinene-dominant in recent vintages. Examples in catalog: Fruit-6, Fruit-9, Fruit-30, Fruit-131, Fruit-132, Fruit-135, Fruit-136, Fruit-137, Fruit-505.
Strategic note: TBF positions fruit-forward Pinene-dominant profiles as "head and shoulders better than what we have seen in the market" (per 2023 About page).

### ⛽ Gas
Diesel / fuel notes, sesquiterpene-leaning. Examples: Gas-10, Gas-41, Gas-151, Gas-152, Gas-157, Gas-707.
Best applications: premium vape carts where caryophyllene-class thermal stability matters.

### 🌲 Pine
Coniferous, alpha-pinene + sesquiterpene mixes. Examples: Pine-1, Pine-2, Pine-37, Pine-71, Pine-121, Pine-125, Pine-129, Pine-606.
Note: Pine compounds have lower flash points (~155-160°C); use cold-process applications.

### 🍰 Dessert
Sweet, baked-good notes; Candies category. Examples: Dessert-45, Dessert-101, Dessert-109, Dessert-110, Dessert-111, Dessert-116, Dessert-154, Dessert-701.
2024 was a "heavy dessert outlook" per About page.

### 🍬 Sweet
Confectionery, candy notes. Examples: Sweet-16, Sweet-62, Sweet-161, Sweet-164, Sweet-602, Sweet-710.

### 🍋 Sour / Citrus
Limonene-forward, beverage-friendly. Examples: Citrus-7, Citrus-78, Citrus-142, Citrus-144, Sour-96.

### 🌶 Savory
Earthy, herbaceous. Examples: Savory-3, Savory-84.

### 🟣 Purple (specialty category)
Recent additions. Examples: Purple-101, Purple-103.

## Product-type taxonomy (used in marketing copy)
1. **Native Cannabis Terpenes** — flagship; full-spectrum, single-vintage, solventless, "Fresh Never Frozen®"
2. **Enhanced Natural / Botanical Blends** — mid-tier; selectively reconstructed for water-compatibility or heat-stability
3. **Type III Genetics Exclusive Oils** — limited-edition vintage releases (the "wine vintage" play)

## Application-specific notes (for content references)
- 510-thread cartridges → early-season monoterpene profiles
- Ceramic-core vape hardware → late-season sesquiterpene-rich profiles
- Distillate carriers → tolerate higher terpene loads than refined oils
- Pre-emulsified delivery → required for beverages
- Lipid-based delivery → preferred for premium cold-process edibles
